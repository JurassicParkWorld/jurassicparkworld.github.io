FAQ

Here you will find Answers to some Questions that have been asked.

Q: Why did you create Jurassic Park World?

A: We started JPW to bring other like minded plays together in a community based group. We want to make it fun for everyone of all ages.

Q: What dose Jurassic Park World have to offer in regards to helping its players?

A: We will offer support for the games with problems, issues. We will also encourage other members of the JPW community to share inputs and try and help out others when they can or have had the same problem. most of the time other players may have faced an issues and was able to fix it and could help out the member who is having that issue.

Q: Will JPW offer support for cracked/hacked versions of the games?

A: No. JPW support team will not offer help on any cracked / hacked version of the games. If any of our members have them they can help if they want. These versions of the games are a use at your own risk.
